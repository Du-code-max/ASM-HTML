Welcome to CHILLLY – the destination for drink lovers!

Here, you will find fragrant milk teas, rich coffees, fresh smoothies and refreshing lemon teas, all made with fresh ingredients and full of passion. More than just drinks, CHILLLY also offers a relaxing space where you can chat, work, or simply spend time for yourself.

From passion to reality – CHILLLY's journey

started from the desire to create a friendly and close place, where people can enjoy a delicious drink together and share everyday stories. We believe that a delicious drink does not only come from the recipe, but also from the heart of the bartender.

Fresh ingredients – Drink with confidence

We use fresh fruit every day, pure tea leaves, clean coffee, and carefully controlled toppings. Every step is done by hand to preserve the full flavor and no preservatives are used – ensuring every glass is absolutely safe and delicious.

For You – Whoever You Are

Whether you are a student who needs a place to study in groups, an office worker who wants to relax, or a young person who wants to "live virtual" with friends - CHILLLY is always ready to welcome you. With reasonable prices, dedicated service and a youthful space, you will always feel comfortable every time you visit.
